Kiet Nguyen is a research scientist affiliated with both Division 25 of UES and the Functional Materials Division of the Materials and Manufacturing Directorate at the Air Force Research Laboratory.  Kiet received a BS in chemistry in 1988, from North Dakota State University, and a PhD in physical chemistry in 1994, from Iowa State University; his PhD advisor was [Mark Gordon](https://www.msg.chem.iastate.edu/).
